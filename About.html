About Adrian Gomez

Hi there! I'm Adrian Gomez, a licensed electrician based right here in the heart of Alaska. I’ve been working in the electrical trade for over 9 years, and I’m proud to offer honest, high-quality electrical services to homeowners and businesses throughout our rugged, beautiful state.

Why I Started My Business

After years of working for larger companies, I realized something was missing. Time with my family. Starting my own electrical business wasn’t just a career move, it was a lifestyle choice. I wanted freedom to be there for the people who matter most, and to build something of value in the community I love. Being my own boss has allowed me to stay close to home, raise my kids with strong Alaskan values, and offer my clients the kind of personalized service you just can’t get from a big outfit.

A Bit About Me
When I’m not wiring up a new home or fixing a blown circuit, you’ll probably find me enjoying the outdoors. Living in Alaska means I get to fish, hike, ride four wheelers, and jet ski, and spend time with my family in one of the most beautiful places on Earth. I’m a strong believer in hard work, good coffee, and getting home in time for dinner.

If you're looking for a trustworthy, local electrician who knows the terrain and cares about the community. I'm your guy.

Now let’s get to work!

Skills & Experience

Over the years, I’ve gained a wide range of experience, including:

- Residential and commercial wiring
- Panel upgrades and service changes
- Lighting installation (indoor, outdoor, and custom designs)
- Troubleshooting and repairs

I’m known for being detail-oriented, reliable, and easy to work with. I treat every job like it’s my own home—because that’s the standard I believe my customers deserve.
